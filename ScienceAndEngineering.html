Science and Engineering


Improvements within the computing industry directly advance other industries, allowing them to complete task that would otherwise be impossible!

Computing has made many positive contributions to Science and Engineering such as:

  • Computers with high performance are used to simulate dynamic process in Science and Engineering.
  • Supercomputers have numerous applications in area of Research and Development (R&D).
    • Running vast simulations
  • Topographic images can be created through computers.
    • Satelite images can be converted into height maps of the ground
  • Scientists use computers to plot and analyze data to have a better understanding of earthquakes.
    • Allow us to create better earthquake proof structures.
    • Save lives.
    • Find at risk areas.
